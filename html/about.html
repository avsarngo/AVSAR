Organization Story

Why AVSAR Exists

AVSAR Social Foundation was established with a simple yet powerful belief: every individual deserves an equal opportunity to learn, grow, and build a better future, regardless of their social or economic circumstances. We believe that lasting change begins when communities are empowered with the right opportunities rather than temporary solutions.

Guided by this vision, AVSAR works to bridge gaps in education, healthcare, digital literacy, environmental sustainability, and community development. Through accessible learning, awareness programmes, health initiatives, and volunteer-driven action, the foundation strives to create meaningful and sustainable impact where it is needed most.

Every initiative undertaken by AVSAR reflects a commitment to compassion, transparency, and collaboration. Our goal is not only to address today's challenges but also to empower individuals with the knowledge, confidence, and opportunities needed to create lasting change for themselves, their families, and future generations.

Areas of Impact

Where AVSAR focuses its work, in one glance.

Six connected focus areas that together explain what AVSAR does and who it serves.

Education

Free tuition, mentorship, and learning support that help children build a strong academic foundation.

Healthcare

Health awareness sessions, iron tablet distribution, and nutrition support for families who need it most.

Women Empowerment

Skill-building, confidence, and support systems that help women create stronger, more independent futures.

Digital Literacy

Computer education and practical digital skills that open doors to further learning and employment.

Environment

Community plantation drives and awareness efforts that build habits of environmental stewardship.

Community Welfare

Clothing and food distribution drives that bring dignity and relief to underserved neighborhoods.

Core Values

The principles that guide every program and every interaction.

Founder Message

Shivam Singh Rajawat

Founder, AVSAR Social Foundation

AVSAR Social Foundation works to uplift underprivileged children by creating access to education, mentorship, and community support rooted in dignity and trust.

Founded by Shivam Singh Rajawat, a former BSF officer, the foundation exists to build stronger and more inclusive communities through long-term social action.

Journey Timeline

How AVSAR's work has grown, one initiative at a time.

Founded with a Vision

AVSAR Social Foundation was established to ensure every child and family has access to education, healthcare, and equal opportunities.

Free Education Centre

A free learning centre was established in Saroj Nagar, Bhind to provide tuition, mentorship, computer education, and career guidance.

Community Outreach

Healthcare awareness, iron tablet distribution, food drives, and nutrition programmes expanded the foundation's reach.

Growing Together

Today AVSAR continues to empower children, families, and volunteers while expanding its social impact through education and community participation.

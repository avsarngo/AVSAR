About AVSAR

Creating Opportunities, Empowering Lives, and Building Stronger Communities.

AVSAR Social Foundation is a non-profit organization committed to creating lasting social impact by ensuring that education, healthcare, and opportunities reach those who need them the most. Founded by Shivam Singh Rajawat, a former Border Security Force (BSF) officer, the foundation works with the belief that every individual deserves the chance to learn, grow, and live with dignity, regardless of their background.

  • Providing free education, mentorship, computer literacy, and career guidance through the Free Education Centre in Saroj Nagar, Bhind.
  • Improving community well-being through health awareness campaigns, Iron Tablet Distribution, nutrition support, and food distribution drives.
  • Encouraging sustainable social development through dedicated volunteers, community participation, and transparent initiatives that create measurable impact.

Success Story

Every Child Deserves an Opportunity. Every Opportunity Creates a Better Future.

AVSAR Social Foundation began with a simple belief—that no child's future should be limited by a lack of education, awareness, or opportunity. What started as a small community initiative has grown into a movement where education, healthcare, and social responsibility come together to create meaningful change.

Today, AVSAR is helping children learn with confidence, supporting families through health and awareness programmes, encouraging school enrolment, promoting digital literacy, and bringing communities together through volunteer-driven initiatives. Every classroom, every awareness session, and every act of service is another step toward building a stronger and more empowered society.

"Real success is not measured by the number of lives we touch today, but by the opportunities we create that continue changing lives for years to come."
